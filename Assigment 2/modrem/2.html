what are html entities? list out 5 commonly used HTML entities ?

  1. HTML entities are special codes or characters used to represent reserved characters, symbols, or special characters that have special meaning in HTML.
  2. These entities ensure that such characters are correctly displayed and interpreted by web browsers
  3. < - Represents the less-than symbol (<).
  4. > - Represents the greater-than symbol (>).
  5. " - Represents the double quotation mark (").
