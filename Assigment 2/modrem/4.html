List any 3 ways which help us in improving the accessibility of HTML

mproving the accessibility of HTML involves making design and development choices that enhance the usability and inclusivity of web content for individuals with disabilities.
Here are three ways to improve the accessibility of HTML:

  1. Use Semantic Markup: Utilize semantic HTML elements appropriately to provide a clear structure and meaning to the content
  2. Provide Alternative Text for Images: Ensure that all images have descriptive alternative text (alt text) specified using the alt attribute
  3. Additionally, it is important to follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C).
